  • Concept

    This game belongs to the genre of roguelites, which is essentially a 2D platformer. The objective for the player is to clear each level, which includes a challenging boss fight that must be defeated in order to progress to the next stage. The game revolves around the theme of swords, with all weapons being sword-related, such as katana, greatsword, needle, knife, longsword, rapier, dagger, and more. Throughout the game, the player can acquire various upgrades such as damage boost, speed enhancement, and special skills. Each level presents a distinct theme, ranging from medieval to Japanese samurai to cyberpunk, thereby influencing the types of enemies and boss fights that the player will encounter.

    Fundamentals

    -The player's health is represented by a health bar, which diminishes with each attack received. If the health bar reaches zero, the player loses the game.

    -There is also a "WILL" bar in the game, which serves as a determining factor for the player's ability to unleash their sword's special ability. The special ability can only be activated if the player's will bar is sufficiently charged.

    -There is a "currency" that can be found scattered throughout the levels, which the player can collect and subsequently spend at randomly placed shops located throughout the stages.

    -There is a score system in place that calculates the player's score based on three factors: the number of hits taken, the amount of damage dealt, and the currency collected.
